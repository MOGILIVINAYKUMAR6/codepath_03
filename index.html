The informal workforce is the backbone of many economies, yet struggles with mental health often go unnoticed. Let's change that.

About the Issue

The informal workforce often lacks access to mental health resources, benefits, and protections that formal workers enjoy. Financial instability, poor working conditions, and lack of job security all contribute to mental health problems among these workers. This website aims to shine a light on these challenges and provide helpful information to those in need.
